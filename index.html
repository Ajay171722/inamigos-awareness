About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation, founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, Founder & CEO, is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization based in Chhattisgarh working across India to create sustainable social impact.


The foundation is recognized with 80G & 12A certification, CSR-1 registration, NITI Aayog registration and ISO 9001:2015 certification, ensuring transparency, quality and accountability.


Through SEVA, BACHPANSHALA, JEEV, UDAAN, PRAKRITI and VIKAS, InAmigos supports education, food distribution, animal welfare, women empowerment, environment protection and youth development.


With volunteers, interns and partners, InAmigos believes in collective action to build an inclusive, compassionate and empowered society.
